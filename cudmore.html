Jamie Cudmore

Team: Oyonnax

Biography:


Jamie Cudmore is a Canadian rugby union player currently playing for the Top 14 club Oyonnax. Previously Cudmore played for
Llandovery RFC, Llanelli RFC, FC Grenoble and ASM Clermont Auvergne. He has been nicknamed 'Cuddles' by his team mates, an ironic moniker
considering Cudmore's tough and ruthless style of play
