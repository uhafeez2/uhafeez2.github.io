Hyun-soo Kim

Team: LG Twins (KBO League)

Biography:


Hyun-soo Kim is a South Korean professional baseball outfielder for the LG Twins of the KBO League.
He previously played for the Doosan Bears, and has also played in Major League Baseball (MLB) for the Baltimore Orioles and Philadelphia Phillies.

In 2016, Kim and Under Armour reached an agreement in which UA agreed to provide Kim with cleats, batting gloves, training wear, and shoes.
