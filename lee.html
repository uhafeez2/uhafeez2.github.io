Marqise Lee

Team: Jacksonville Jaguars

Biography:


Marqise Lee is an American football wide receiver for the Jacksonville Jaguars of the National Football League (NFL).
He was drafted by the Jaguars in the second round of the 2014 NFL Draft. He played college football at USC, where he was the 2012 Biletnikoff Award
winner as the nation's top receiver and a unanimous All-American.

In 2014, Marqise Lee joined the Under Armour family.
