Andre Williams

Team: LA Chargers

Biography:


Andre Rishard Williams is an American football running back for the Los Angeles Chargers of the National Football League (NFL).
He was drafted by the New York Giants in the fourth round of the 2014 NFL Draft. He played college football at Boston College, where he was a finalist
for the 2013 Heisman Trophy, and won the 2013 Doak Walker Award as the nation's best running back. During the 2013 season, Williams became only the
16th player in NCAA history to rush for over 2,000 yards and he finished his college career ranked 5th all-time for most yards rushed in a single season
with 2,177 yards.

In 2014, Andre Williams signed with Under Armour.
