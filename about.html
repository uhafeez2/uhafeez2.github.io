About

Note: All of the information on this website is correct as of January 1st, 2018

As an avid sports fan and someone who regularly participates in team sports (mainly basketball), over the years, I have developed an enthusiasm towards
brands who are at the pinnacle of today's activewear and performance based products. One brand in particular that I have supported for many years
and is taking the sports and fitness world by storm is Under Armour. Not only has Under Armour continued to raise the bar with every single product launch
since its founding in 1996, but it has also done a magnificent and praiseworthy job in scouting the next wave of elite sports talent, and offering them sponsorship deals.
Personally, I highly enjoy watching Under Armour athletes such as Stephen Curry of the Golden State Warriors, as well as newly signed rookie sensation Dennis Smith Jr.
I am also a very big fan of the Under Armour Association (UAA), which is Under Armour's basketball AAU circuit, tailored towards the top high school players.
Some of my favorite NBA players who played for the UAA in the past include Milwaukee Bucks player Thon Maker, and Phoenix Suns Forward Josh Jackson,
who was selected as the fourth pick of the 2017 NBA draft by the Phoenix Suns.

With all that being said, I wanted to develop a website that displays the great diversity of Under Armour athletes and showcases the talent that separates
UA from other competitor brands. This site serves as a database containing an ever-growing list of astounding athletes that are a part of the Under Armour family.
In the "Home" page, you will find a list of Under Armour athletes on which you can click on any of the names to learn more about that particular athlete.
Furthermore, in the "Gallery" page, you will find some of my personal favorite pictures of some UA athletes and products. Enjoy!
