Nick Markakis

Team: Atlanta Braves

Biography:


Nicholas William Markakis is an American professional baseball right fielder for the Atlanta Braves of Major League Baseball (MLB).
He previously played for the Baltimore Orioles from 2006 to 2014. Markakis is a two-time Gold Glove Award winner.

Nick Markakis agreed to a deal with Under Armour in 2011.
