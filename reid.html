Eric Reid

Team: San Francisco 49ers

Biography:


Eric Todd Reid is an American football strong safety for the San Francisco 49ers of the National Football League (NFL).
He played college football for Louisiana State University (LSU), and received consensus All-American recognition. He was selected by the 49ers in the first
round of the 2013 NFL Draft.
