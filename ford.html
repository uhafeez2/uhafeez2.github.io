Dee Ford

Team: Kansas City Chiefs

Biography:


Dee Ford is an American football outside linebacker for the Kansas City Chiefs of the National Football League (NFL).
He was drafted by the Chiefs in the first round of the 2014 NFL Draft. He played college football at Auburn.

In 2014, Ford signed an apparel deal with Under Armour.
