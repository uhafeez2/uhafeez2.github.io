Brandon Marshall

Team: New York Giants

Biography:


Brandon Tyrone Marshall is an American football wide receiver for the New York Giants of the National Football League (NFL).
He played college football at UCF, and was drafted by the Denver Broncos in the fourth round of the 2006 NFL Draft. Marshall has also played for the Miami Dolphins,
Chicago Bears and New York Jets. Marshall is known for his ability to break and dodge tackles. He led all NFL wide receivers in yards after first contact
for the 2007 NFL season. Regarding Marshall's breakaway ability, cornerback Brandon Flowers said, "Brandon Marshall is a defensive lineman playing wide
receiver. He wants to inflict punishment on you. He wants you to try to tackle him so he can shove you off of him and get more yards."
Cornerback Nnamdi Asomugha said Marshall is 'the toughest guy to bring down, one-on-one.'

In 2014, Marshall signed with Under Armour.
