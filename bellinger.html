Cody Bellinger

Team: LA Dodgers

Biography:


Cody James Bellinger is an American professional baseball first baseman and outfielder for the Los Angeles Dodgers of Major League Baseball (MLB).
He made his MLB debut with the Dodgers in 2017. He was named an MLB All-Star and the National League's Rookie of the Year. Bellinger is the son of Clay Bellinger,
who also played in MLB.
