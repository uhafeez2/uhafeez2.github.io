Vance McDonald

Team: Pittsburgh Steelers

Biography:


Vance Coman McDonald is an American football tight end for the Pittsburgh Steelers of the National Football League (NFL).
He was drafted by the San Francisco 49ers in the second round of the 2013 NFL Draft. He played college football at Rice.
