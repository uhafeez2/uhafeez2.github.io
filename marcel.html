Marcel Hirscher

Title: Ski Racer

Biography:


Marcel Hirscher is an Austrian World Cup alpine ski racer. Hirscher made his World Cup debut in March 2007.
He competes primarily in slalom and giant slalom, as well as combined and occasionally in super G. Winner of a record six consecutive World Cup titles,
Hirscher has also won nine medals at the Alpine Skiing World Championships (six gold) and a silver medal in slalom at the 2014 Winter Olympics.

In 2015, Marcel and UA agreed to a contract extension. "I am super excited to be a part of the Under Armour team. I really like the brand and the
products work great. I'm looking forward to our upcoming projects", said Marcel Hirscher.
