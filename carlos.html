Carlos Cuadras

Title: Professional boxer

Biography:


Carlos Roberto Cuadras Quiroa is a Mexican professional boxer in the Super Flyweight division who is the former WBC
Super Flyweight Champion. Cuadras also was a Gold Medalist in the Bantamweight division at the 2007 Pan American Games.
