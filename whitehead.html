Isaiah Whitehead

Team: Brooklyn Nets

Biography:


Isaiah Whitehead is a professional basketball player for the Brooklyn Nets of the National Basketball Association (NBA).
Whitehead played college basketball for Seton Hall. On June 23, 2016, Whitehead was selected by the Utah Jazz with the 42nd overall pick in the 2016 NBA draft.
He was later traded to the Brooklyn Nets the following day. On July 8, 2016, he signed with the Nets and joined the team for the 2016 NBA Summer League.
In September 2016, Isaiah Whitehead officially signed a contract with Under Armour.
